Now

My current focus, as of June 13, 2020, is on:

  1. Start expanding my reach in the FOSS community
  2. Running

I keep score of my past areas of focus in the retrospectives page.

To the imaginary friend that I haven’t seen in a while, asking me how it’s going

I’ve been working in a new company, almost for 2 months now, and it’s great.

I’m still trying to become a good engineer. To do that, I’m now trying to find projects that I can release as FOSS. With that in place, I’d like to build one talk around them to present in some conference in the future. There’s a Meetup that one of my colleagues organizes in Tartu, which can be a good first stage to collect and test ideas.

I’ve read something about re-reading books, and this time around sounded convincing. I’m re-reading an old book I bought 10 years ago, Humboldt’s Gift by Saul Bellow, and I’m having much fun. The idea of writing some (short) fiction is nagging at me but it’s still a kind of effort that I don’t feel like putting in right now.

I’m slowly getting back to a pre-COVID-like life. Had my hair cut today after 3 months and 5 days. I’m running. I’m not going to bars and restaurant yet, unless they have an open terrace. I don’t plan to visit Italy soon.
