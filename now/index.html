Now

What's happening right now, as of March 7, 2021.

I've been working in Glia for 10 months already. It's still great!

My life-long journey to becoming a good engineer is currently focused on prepping up for feature leading and evangelism.

Feature leading is a big experiment in holistic thinking, planning, and leading the development of a small feature and I'm very excited about it and humbled by the fact I'm allowed to experiment. This should happen in either Q2 or Q3.

On the side of evangelism, I just finished a first blog post on my initial experience with Elixir, about to get published. In the longer run, I intend to attend conferences and speak about interesting projects, but that's still far on the horizon.

I'm not running at all. I don't have much in the way of excuses, I'm just doing it less, at least for the winter.

The situation with the COVID-19 is rather bad. I haven't been in the office since last November or so. Estonia is facing the highest infection rate ever since the start of the pandemic. Temperatures are still around- or sub-zero even though there's much more sun. Vaccinations proceed slowly. I hope I'll get my shot(s) by mid-Summer. I'm not going to bars or restaurants, just walking around. I plan to visit Italy, but not sure how soon.
